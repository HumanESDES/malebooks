Send eBooks to Mali

In Mali, one can get a University degree without ever reading a novel.

Yet, reading develops openness, writing skills, tolerance, curiosity and much more. It's an incredible personnal development factor.

In a nutshell

  • Crowdfunding campaign and partnerships.
  • 30 eReaders procurement.
  • eReaders feeding with :
    • 2 500 public domain and free books (french classics).
    • Wikipedia in French
    • Shorts and novels from amateur writers (from France and Mali).
  • eReaders and solar kit shipments to Mali.
  • Identification of recipients (schools and individuals).
  • Recipients training in Bamako.
  • Report on usages, efficiency and scalability.

What you can do

We obviously need your help to pursue this great project:

  • Talk about it and share the link on your social networks: Malebooks, Twitter.
  • Participate (donation or publicity) in our crowdfunding campaign to fund the equiments.
  • Contact french amateur writers to convince them to donate copies of their novels and shorts for the ereaders.
